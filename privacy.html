Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. Your privacy is our priority.

1. No Data Collection

CSB (Chrome Speed Booster) is built on the principle of local processing. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or usage statistics.

2. Local Processing

All optimization processes (tab suspension, memory trimming) happen directly on your machine within the browser's sandbox. No data ever leaves your device.

3. Permissions

CSB requires only the necessary permissions to function:

  • Tabs: To detect idle tabs and manage their state.
  • Storage: To save your local settings (e.g., whitelist).

4. Transparency

Since our code is Open Source, anyone can audit the extension to verify that no malicious activity or data tracking is occurring.
